# Social Signals Notes

You asked for NightScout to always look through social media and websites for specials, events, food, and alcohol.

## Best realistic approach
Use a layered approach:

1. Google business data
2. Venue official website
3. Venue-provided event feed / dashboard
4. Approved platform APIs where access is allowed
5. Manual moderation for high-value venues

## Why this matters
A direct promise to "always scan social media" is risky because platforms limit or change access.
The safer product wording is:

"NightScout checks venue websites, approved business feeds, and latest posted signals when available."

## Recommended NightScout UI copy
- "Latest venue signals"
- "Reported specials"
- "Website-posted events"
- "Venue-updated tonight"
